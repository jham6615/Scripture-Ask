Common questions

  • How do I change translations? Tap the version label under the book name at the top, then pick a language and translation.
  • How does the free plan work? You get 5 AI questions per day for free. Beacon Premium removes the daily cap.
  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? On your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Beacon Bible.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Open the Premium screen and tap "Restore purchases."
  • How do I delete my account? Email us and we'll remove your account and data.
